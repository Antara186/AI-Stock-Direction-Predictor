# Technical Indicators Used

## RSI (Relative Strength Index)

Measures market momentum and identifies overbought or oversold conditions.

## SMA (Simple Moving Average)

Calculates average stock price over a fixed period.

## EMA (Exponential Moving Average)

Gives higher importance to recent prices.

## MACD

Measures trend strength and momentum.

## Volatility

Measures market risk and price fluctuations.
